1. Introduction 

We present below Tick and Dash privacy policy which will help you understand what type of personal data we collect, your personal choices regarding these practices, and how it will be used and shared. When you trust us with your personal information and data, we plan to keep that trust, and that's why we will help you understand our privacy practices and guidelines.  

II. 0verview 

  1. Scope

These privacy practices will be applied to all users of Tick and Dash’s services anywhere that includes the company apps, services, and websites, and it's used explicitly to:

  • The riders: who receive or request the transportation.
  • The drivers: who provide the transportation to riders.

Anywhere in this note, the word "users" refers to all those subject to the policy.

Furthermore, please note that for Guest Users: The personal data of those who request or receive transportation via the manual booking machines (such as when ordering transportation from the main station) may be used for purposes described in this notice.

  1. Data controller 

Tick and Dash for apps Inc control all the personal data that Tick and Dash’s services collect anywhere in the world.

III. Data collection and uses
  1. The data we collect 
  • Data provided by the users of Tick and Dash when they create or update an account
  • Data created during the use of Tick and Dash services, such as app usage, location, and device data
  • Other sources of data, such as Tick and Dash partners and third parties that use Tick and Dash’s APIs

We present below details about the data that are collected by or on behalf of Tick and Dash:

  • The data that the users of Tick and Dash provide
  • User profile, we collect data when users create and update their Tick and Dash profile; which may include their name, phone number, email, login name and password, profile picture, address, payment, or banking information (that include all related payment verification information), driver's license and other identification documents (like birth date, photo, ID, and gender). Users also provide vehicle or insurance information of drivers, permit number, the care model, emergency contact information, user settings, and services using Tick and Dash's app. They also provide occupation and gender (when required for certain Tick and Dash services or programs, such as Tick and Dash Wallet. For more information, please read "How we use personal data" Section.
  • Background check, we may do background checks for Tick and Dash drivers. This may include information like the criminal record or driver history (if it’s permitted by law) and the permit to work as a public transportation driver. An authorized vendor may collect these personal data on Tick and Dash’s behalf.
  • Demographic data, we collect demographic data about users, which include user surveys. And sometimes, we may also receive demographic data about users from third parties.
  • User content, we may also collect the information users submit when they contact our customer support, provide ratings or compliments for other users. This may include photographs, feedback, or other recordings collected by users of Tick and Dash.

 

  • The data created during the use of Tick and Dash services 

 

  • Location data (driver): Tick and Dash collect this data when the Tick and Dash app is running in the foreground (on-screen) or background (not on-screen) of their mobile device.
  • Location data (riders). We collect precise and approximate location data from riders’ mobile devices when they enable us to do so. Tick and Dash collect this data from the time the rider requests a ride until it is finished and whenever the app is running in the foreground of Tick and Dash user’s mobile device. We use this data to enhance our apps, improve our pick-up stations' locations, enable safety features, and prevent and detect fraud attempts.
  • Tick and Dash collect driver location data and link location data collected during a trip with their rider's profile; even if the rider has not permitted us to collect location data from their device, it enables us to offer services to riders, like invoice generation and customer support.
  • Usage data:  Tick and Dash collects data about how users interact with our products and services. This includes data like access dates and times, pages viewed or app features, app crashes and system activity, the type of browser that is being used, and third-party sites or services used before interacting with our services. In some cases, we collect this data through cookies, tags, pixels, and similar tracking technologies that create unique identifiers.
  • Transaction information: We collect information related to the use of our services, including the date and time, type of services requested or provided, distance traveled, the chosen station, amount charged, and the payment method used. Furthermore, when someone uses any promotional code that the company provided to you, we will associate your name with that person.
  • Communications data: We enable users to communicate with Tick and Dash and with each other on Tick and Dash’s mobile app and websites. For example, we help drivers and riders to request or receive rides (generally without disclosing their telephone numbers to each other). Tick and Dash may also use the data for customer support services (to resolve disputes between users), for safety and security purposes, to improve our products and services, and for analytics.
  • Device data: We may collect data like the devices used to access our services, including device IP address, hardware models, operating systems and versions of that system, software used, preferred user languages, advertising unique identifiers, device identifiers, serial numbers, mobile network data, and device motion data.

  • Users participating in the referral programs, like when a user refers another person, we collect the referred person's data from that user.
  • The feedbacks, ratings, reviews, and complaints that the users provide when they use our services 
  • We may collect data from the users or others in case of disputes and complaints. 
  • Other Vendors who will help us verify users’ identity, eligibility to work, and background information, for regulatory, safety, and security uses.
  • Other public transportation companies, insurance companies, marketing and financial services providers.

IV. Choices and company transparency

We value our users’ privacy and allow users to adjust and control the data that we collect through one of the following ways;

  • In-app settings and permissions 
  • Marketing communications opt-outs

Please read below about how users can adjust and control their privacy settings when they use Tick and Dash app;

  1. In-app settings and permissions 

 

  • Users can adjust their privacy preferences by going to privacy settings menus in Tick and Dash app to control their preferred locations and app notifications.  
  • Users can request a copy of their personal data that the company collects.
  • The ability to use prebuilt device platform permissions (IOS and Android); Which will help Tick and Dash users to control the data they share with Tick and Dash.

 

  1. Marketing communications opt-outs

 

  • Users can adjust marketing and promotional messages and emails that they receive from Tick and Dash by following unsubscribe instructions that we include in these messages. However, we may still send to our users non promotional and communication messages or emails that help us to provide our users with more reliable services.
